About

'Hack_Out CTF' is a non-commercial wargame platform designed for enjoyment, offering diverse pwn challenges related to system exploitation. The primary objective of Hack_Out is to provide an engaging and entertaining experience for participants, treating each challenge as a game.

How-to play

For each challenge on 'Hack_Out CTF,' there are flag files akin to Capture The Flag (CTF) contests. Your task is to read these files and submit them to your Dashboard to earn points. To successfully extract the flag, you must employ skills in Steganography, Network Hacking, system understanding, cryptography, and Code Review. Each challenge comes with an intended solution crafted by the author, but there are also numerous unintended solutions to explore. The challenges are categorized into Six distinct areas.

Challenges

  • Open Doors- Find open ports {Very easy challenge}
  • Rookiss (Anonymous) - Find your First Flag {Easy Challenge}
  • Grotesque (Code Review) - User Login {Medium Challenge}
  • Hacker's Secret (Steganography) - Find Secret Username {Medium Challenge}
  • Break the Chamber (Brute force) - Find Secret Password {Medium Challenge}
  • MailerMail (File Finding) - Find root Mail & root password {Very easy challenge}
  • Bingo RootFlag - Find root flag {Very easy challenge}
